# Account — Facts & Rules

## Account Status

| Status | Meaning | Agent can do |
|---|---|---|
| Active | Normal access | Full support |
| Suspended | Temporary restriction — can't place orders or initiate returns | Explain reason, direct to appeals |
| Banned | Permanent restriction | Cannot process orders/returns |

Suspended accounts: existing orders in transit are still fulfilled.

## What Agents Can See / Cannot See
Can see: name, email, phone, order history, return history, loyalty points/tier, account status, saved addresses
Cannot see: full card numbers (last 4 only), password, 2FA codes

## Actions Agents Cannot Do
- Reset passwords or change email (customer self-serves or escalate for identity verification)
- Unsuspend or unban accounts (trust & safety team only)
- Generate data exports (formal request via email required)
- Process account deletion via chat (must email support@leafy.store)

## Suspension Triggers
Suspected payment fraud | Chargeback without contacting support first | Returning 80%+ of orders over 3+ months | Terms violation

## Ban Triggers
Confirmed fraud | Serial return abuse after warnings | Harassment of support staff | Multiple fraudulent chargebacks

## Appeals
Email support@leafy.store — subject: "Account Appeal — [email or order ID]"
Reviewed within 5 business days. Banned accounts: one appeal, decision is final.

## Common Requests
- Forgot password → direct to "Forgot Password" on login page (reset link expires in 30 min)
- Delete account → email support@leafy.store subject "Account Deletion Request — [email]". Data anonymised in 30 days, order records kept 7 years.
- Marketing unsubscribe → link in any email, or Account Settings → Notifications. Takes 48h. Does not affect transactional emails.
- Data export → email support@leafy.store subject "Data Export Request — [email]". Processed in 30 days.
- Two accounts? → Not allowed. One per email. Duplicates may be merged or suspended.
