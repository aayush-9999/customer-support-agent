# Cancellation Policy — Rules

## Cancellation Window

| Order Status | Can Cancel? | Outcome if Yes |
|---|---|---|
| Processing | Yes | Full refund incl. original shipping |
| Shipped / In Transit / Out for Delivery | No | Must wait for delivery, then return |
| Delivered | No | Return within return window |
| Already Cancelled | N/A | Confirm + verify refund was issued |

Speed matters — orders move from Processing to Shipped within hours on business days.

## Refund on Cancellation
- Full refund including original shipping cost
- To original payment method (3–5 business days) or store credit instantly (no bonus, unlike returns)

## Partial Cancellations
- Individual items can be cancelled if the order has not yet been packed
- Once packing begins → partial cancellation not possible. Customer must return unwanted item after delivery.

## Leafy-Initiated Cancellations
| Reason | Resolution |
|---|---|
| Payment failed after 24h hold | Full refund — customer must reorder |
| Item out of stock after order | Full refund automatically |
| Suspected fraud | Contact support to verify identity |
| Address undeliverable | Full refund — customer must reorder |
| Customer in restricted region | Full refund |

## Pre-orders & Backorders
- Pre-ordered items: cancellable any time before shipment
- Backordered items: standard cancellation rules from the point of shipment. 2-hour grace period after ship notification email.
