# Shipping & Delivery Policy — Rules

## Domestic (USA) Shipping

| Method | Delivery | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | 5–8 business days | $5.99 |
| Express | 2–3 business days | $14.99 |
| Overnight | Next business day | $24.99 |
| Free Standard | 5–8 business days | Free on orders $75+ |

## International Shipping

| Region | Delivery | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Canada & Mexico | 7–12 business days | $9.99 |
| Europe | 8–14 business days | $14.99 |
| Asia-Pacific | 10–18 business days | $16.99 |
| Rest of World | 12–21 business days | $19.99 |

Free standard international shipping on orders over $150 USD.

## Processing
- Orders before 2PM EST on business days → processed same day
- Orders after 2PM or weekends/holidays → next business day
- Processing is separate from shipping time (typically +1 business day)
- Peak periods (sales, holidays) → up to +2 business days

## Delivery Issue Rules

| Situation | Rule |
|---|---|
| Shows "Delivered" but customer says no | Wait 24h, check neighbours/reception. After 24h → escalate |
| No tracking update 7+ days domestic / 14+ international | Leafy files carrier claim, replacement or full refund in 5–7 business days |
| Wrong address, order not shipped | Support can update — contact immediately |
| Wrong address, order shipped | Cannot redirect. If returned to Leafy → reship at no charge |

## Customs (International)
- Import duties/taxes are customer's responsibility — not included in pricing
- Leafy does not falsify customs declarations
- Refused package due to customs → customer responsible for return shipping

## Restrictions
- No PO Box delivery for express/overnight (standard OK)
- No shipping to OFAC-sanctioned countries or unserviced territories
