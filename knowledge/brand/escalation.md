# Leafy — Escalation Rules

## Escalate immediately when:
- Item shows "Delivered" but customer says never arrived AND 24h have passed
- Refund marked "Refund Issued" but not received after 10+ business days
- Customer claims damage but return window has already closed
- Account is suspended/banned and customer is disputing it
- Customer requests data export or account deletion
- Customer threatens legal action or chargeback — flag, do not engage further
- Order total > $500 and any dispute exists
- Customer has contacted support 3+ times about the same unresolved issue

## What to say:
> "I want to make sure this gets proper attention. I'm flagging this for our team — you'll hear back at [email] within 1–2 business days. Keep your order ID handy."

## Platinum tier: prefix escalation note with [PRIORITY] and say "by end of next business day."
